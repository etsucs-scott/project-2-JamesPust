## WAR Instructions
To play the game of war first you deal out the entite 52 card deck. 
Then each player(2-4) flips a card from the top of their deck and everyone compares cards.
After each player has flipped a card then the player with the highest value card wins.
(Jack = 11, Queen = 12, King = 13, Ace = 14).
The player that wins the rounds then keeps all the cards in that round.
If players have the same highest card then then only the tieing players flip another card.
This keeps happening until someone has a higher card and then that player wins all cards flipped that round.
If you run out cards you are eliminated from the game, and you win once all players are eliminted.
In the case that the game goes more than 10,000 rounds it called and automatic tie.
